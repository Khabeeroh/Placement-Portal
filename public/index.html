Frequently Asked Questions

Who can apply for these program?

Students currently enrolled in a tertiary institution can apply for IT and SIWES programs, while graduates and final-year students are eligible for NYSC placement opportunities.

What is the difference between IT, SIWES, and NYSC?

IT (Industrial Training) and SIWES are internship programs for students to gain practical experience during their studies, while NYSC is a mandatory one-year national service program for graduates in Nigeria.

How long do the programs last?

IT and SIWES placements typically last between 3 to 6 months, depending on your institution’s requirement, while NYSC lasts for 12 months.

Is there a fee to apply?

Application to the placement portal is free. However, some specialized training programs or courses may have associated fees, which will be clearly stated.

What documents are required for application?

Applicants are required to submit documents such as:

  • Student ID or School ID
  • CV/Resume
  • School letter (for IT/SIWES)
  • NYSC call-up letter (for corps members)

How do I apply for a program?

Click on the “Apply Now” button, fill in your personal details, select your preferred program and course, upload required documents, and submit your application.
